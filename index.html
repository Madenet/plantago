About Our Company

Plantago Lanceolata Pty Ltd is a 100% black-owned company with Level 1-BBBEE status, owned by Mr. Divhani Mulaudzi (Prl.Nat. Sci: 007992 and EAPSA Rep: 2019/1184), who holds a Bachelor of Science Honours in Botany from the University of Limpopo.

Our company specializes in environmental authorisation processes, water use licensing, mining permits, town planning, and development rights applications.
